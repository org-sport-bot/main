@org_sport_bot

Track activities, manage participants, and monitor payments — all inside Telegram. No schedules, no reminders, just the essentials.

▶ Launch @org_sport_bot

What It Does

📋 Activity Tracking

Create sports events, set fixed or per-person costs, and log attendance. Simple, text-based records.

👥 Participant Management

Add players via Telegram username. Mark attendance instantly and keep a clean roster.

⚖ Balance & Payments

Auto-calculate who owes what. Track income/expenses per activity or per participant.

📊 Plain-Text Reports

Generate formatted Markdown reports for any period. Ready to copy-paste, no dashboards.

How to Start

1️⃣Open Telegram & find @org_sport_bot
